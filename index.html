Family Unity

Denny believes in the unity of the family and the men in our city. Now is the time to step up and become the men we were designed to be. Support the Police

Denny is a strong supporter of the police, fire fighters and paramedics. He feels there is a lack of respect and support from many people. They protect our cities, our homes, our children and our neighborhoods. They are the ones that respond to crimes, overdoses and raging fires and yet in many cases, they are made out to be the bad guys. He feels we need to raise awareness of their value and importance to our community and safety.

Fiscally Responsible

Denny is a proponent of a balanced budget, control spending, investing our revenues in worthy projects that enhance our community. Our roads, parks and beaches need better upkeep.
